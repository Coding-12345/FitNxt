Warm Up:


A quick and effective 4-5 minute warm-up can include dynamic exercises such as jumping jacks, bodyweight squats, arm circles, high knees, and dynamic lunges. These movements engage major muscle groups, elevate your heart rate, and enhance flexibility, preparing your body for more intense physical activity.
